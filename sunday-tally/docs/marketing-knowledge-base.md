## What is Sunday Tally?

Sunday Tally is a church analytics platform built specifically for ministry teams. It lets churches track any metric they care about, analyze trends over time, and build custom dashboards — all powered by Tally AI.

It is not a church management system (ChMS). It does not manage member records, process giving, or handle scheduling. It is the analytics layer that sits on top of how your church already operates, turning your weekly numbers into clarity.

### Tally AI — Ask Mode
A live AI chat window connected directly to your church's data. Ask any question in plain English and get an instant answer.

Example questions churches ask:
- "How did Easter attendance compare to last year?"
- "What's our volunteer-to-kids ratio been over the last 8 weeks?"
- "Are we trending up or down in first-time guests this quarter?"
- "Which Sunday this year had our highest giving?"
- "How does our small group participation compare to attendance?"

This is not a weekly report. It is not a scheduled email summary. It is a live conversation with your data, available any time, answered in seconds.

### Tally AI — Build Mode
Turn any Tally AI answer into a saved dashboard widget. Ask a question, see the answer visualized as a chart or metric card, and save it to your church's shared widget library with one click.

Once saved, any team member can:
- Pull the widget from the shared library
- Drag it onto their dashboard
- Resize it to fit their layout
- Arrange their entire dashboard however they want

The children's director builds a kids dashboard. The finance team builds a giving dashboard. The lead pastor builds a board-ready overview. All from the same shared library. All built by AI. None requiring a developer, a spreadsheet formula, or a support ticket.

### Shared Widget Library
Every widget built by Tally AI is saved to a shared library accessible to your entire team. Widgets auto-refresh with new data. Build once, use everywhere.

### Custom Dashboards
Drag, drop, and resize widgets from the library into a fully custom dashboard layout. Every team member can arrange their own view. No two dashboards have to look the same.

### Standard Dashboard — Included on Every Plan (Base $22/mo)
Every Sunday Tally plan includes a standard dashboard built around the metrics all churches care about. This is not a blank slate — it is a fully built, data-populated dashboard that gives your team real, actionable numbers from day one. No AI required. No setup beyond entering your numbers.

**Hero KPI Cards (top of dashboard):**
Three at-a-glance cards showing this week vs last week with percentage change indicators:
- Attendance — total weekly headcount vs last week
- Giving — total weekly giving in dollars vs last week
- Serving — total volunteer count vs last week

**Key Metrics Section (auto-calculated):**
Sunday Tally automatically calculates these from your entered counts — no formulas needed:
- Avg Weekly Attendance — current week, 4-week average, YTD, Prior YTD
- Volunteers / Attendance Ratio — current %, 4-week avg, YTD, Prior YTD — includes a target you set and a "met / not met" indicator
- Ministry-specific attendance (e.g. LifeKids / Kids) — current week, 4-week avg, YTD, Prior YTD
- Per-Capita Giving — current $, 4-week avg, YTD, Prior YTD
- Total Volunteers — current week, 4-week avg, YTD, Prior YTD

**Totals Table:**
A structured summary table showing four time periods side by side — Current Week, Last 4-Week, Current YTD, Prior YTD — for:
- Grand Total attendance
- Experience Total (adults)
- Kids / LifeKids Total
- Total Volunteers
- Giving

**Ministry Sections (auto-built, below totals):**
Each ministry your church adds automatically generates its own section in the standard dashboard. No configuration needed — the dashboard builds itself around your church's structure. Each ministry section shows the same four-column time comparison (Curr Wk, Last 4-Wk, Curr YTD, Prior YTD) with percentage change indicators for every metric.

Example ministry sections from a live church:

**The four standard tracking categories — every ministry section includes these:**

- **Attendance** — headcount for that ministry
- **Volunteers** — serving count for that ministry. Volunteers can be broken out by type — cafe team, parking team, kids check-in, worship team, greeting, or whatever volunteer roles your church tracks. Each type is tracked separately and rolls up to a total volunteer count automatically.
- **Giving** — financial totals (typically at the church level)
- **Stats** — a flexible bucket your church defines. Stats is not a fixed list. Each church fills it with whatever counts matter to them: salvations, hands raised, new members, baptisms, first-time guests, cars in the parking lot, rooms open, food pantry visits — anything. You define the Stats for your church and they appear automatically in the dashboard alongside Attendance, Volunteers, and Giving.

Every metric appears in its ministry section with the same four-period time comparison — Current Week, Last 4-Week, Current YTD, Prior YTD — and percentage change indicators. Nothing to configure. Add a metric, it appears.

Add a new ministry and its section appears automatically. Add a new metric to a ministry and it appears in that section automatically. Nothing to build or configure.

**Multi-campus support:**
The dashboard includes a campus selector — view all campuses rolled up together or filter to a single campus. Date navigation lets you jump to any week.

## What Sunday Tally Is Not

- **Not a ChMS** — Sunday Tally does not manage member records, process giving, or handle check-ins. It is an analytics layer, not a management system.
- **Not an individual attendance tracker** — Sunday Tally tracks counts, not individuals. It does not record who attended, only how many.
- **Not a giving platform** — Sunday Tally tracks giving totals as a metric. It does not process donations.
- **Not a scheduling tool** — Sunday Tally does not manage volunteer schedules or service planning.

If your church uses Planning Center, Breeze, Tithe.ly, or another ChMS, Sunday Tally works alongside it — not instead of it.

## How Sunday Tally Compares

### vs. Church Metrics (Gloo)
Church Metrics is free and widely used. It handles basic attendance and giving tracking with fixed categories and scheduled reports. Sunday Tally adds open-ended custom metric tracking, a live AI conversation layer, and an AI-powered dashboard builder that Church Metrics does not have. If your church has outgrown a basic tracking tool and wants to actually ask questions of your data, Sunday Tally is the next step.

### vs. Holy Insights
Holy Insights offers a fixed set of dashboard categories and an AI that delivers scheduled coaching reports. Sunday Tally tracks any metric your ministry defines (not a preset list), and Tally AI is a live conversation with your data — not a weekly summary email. Sunday Tally also includes the AI dashboard builder and shared widget library, which Holy Insights does not offer.

### vs. Spreadsheets
Spreadsheets require manual formula building, don't talk back, can't build dashboards, and break when someone updates the wrong cell. Sunday Tally replaces the spreadsheet entirely — your team enters numbers, Tally AI handles the analysis, and the dashboard builds itself.
