# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Church leaders, pastors, and administrators who are frustrated with outdated spreadsheets and want instant, AI-driven data analysis of their church metrics.

## Product Purpose
To serve as an "Engineered Record" that imports years of church history instantly and builds custom analytical dashboards on command, eliminating the need for manual spreadsheet calculations.

## Brand Personality
Engineered, crisp, Stark, neutral, suddenly vibrant.

## Anti-references
Cluttered AI "magic" interfaces, overly warm or generic SaaS templates, bloated dashboards with too many unrequested charts.

## Design Principles
- Stark neutrality: Keep things aggressively black, white, and FAFAFA gray until interacted with.
- The "Reveal": Color should be earned through interaction (hovers, focus, data presentation).
- Focus on the numbers: The data is the primary visual element; let it speak clearly without decorative noise.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
Maintain high contrast in the stark neutral state. Ensure interactive color bursts meet contrast standards against their background.
